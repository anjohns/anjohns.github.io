Outside of Work

I’m a proud father, and just about all of my free time revolves around that role. We spend our weekends testing out new playgrounds, walking the museums, and getting ice cream. I know these are the salad days.

Small engines and mowing: I’ve been learning a lot about small engines. A kind neighbor offloaded a decades-old commercial push mower, a Yazoo Master Mower. Yazoo went under years ago and parts are rare. I’m learning to keep it alive. It’s loud, heavy, and dangerous, which makes mowing a thrill. Who knew carburetors could be so interesting?
