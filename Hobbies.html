Hobbies

I’m a proud father, and these days just about all of my free time revolves around that role. We spend our weekends testing out new playgrounds, roaming the children’s museum, and getting ice cream. Nothing fancy—just the simple, fantastic stuff that feels like the whole point.

On the side, I’ve been learning a lot about small engines. A kind neighbor offloaded a decades-old industrial push mower, and I’m figuring out how to keep it alive. It’s loud, heavy, dangerous, and rewarding to work on. Who knew carburetors could be so interesting?
